Emanuele Chiusaroli, Founder & CEO of Handy Signs, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
Handy Signs has developed the first AI-powered application for translating Italian Sign Language (LIS). The market for Handy Signs is the assistive technology market, which worldwide was around €24B and in Europe was around €2.5B, and Handy Signs aims to reach a market share of €15M in 3 years.
