Gilberto Mayor, CEO of Beegol, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
The problem that we solve is autonomous network and AI operations for large ISPs. We do everything using AI and autonomously so that we can decrease OPEX while increasing customer satisfaction and revenue generation. Imagine that the total OPEX for ISPs for network maintenance, customer care, and operations is around $200B worldwide, excluding China. And if AI can save 25% of this OPEX, we’re talking about a $50B market per year. So it’s basically: How can we automate operations that identify the problems; either solve them or send the right robot ticket for people to solve them; and talk with the customer automatically?
