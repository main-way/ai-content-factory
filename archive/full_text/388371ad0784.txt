Eduardo Garcia, Founder and CEO of Moozz, tells CB Insights tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
Moozz operates at the intersection of global recorded music, AI voice technology, and media localization. We are building a new category: AI-powered music language adaptation infrastructure. This enables songs to be released in multiple languages while preserving the original artist’s voice, emotion, and musical integrity. Unlike film and TV, music has remained structurally monolingual. We unlock that last barrier. According to the International Federation of Phonographic Industry, global recorded music revenues exceed $29B annually, driven primarily by streaming. With over 120,000 daily uploads, 300M tracks available, and 50M creators worldwide, even modest multilingual adaptation represents a multi-billion-dollar annual opportunity. Additionally, the global media localization market exceeds $4B annually, yet music remains largely untapped due to technical complexity. We conservatively estimate Moozz’s total addressable market at $5 to $10B annually across labels, distributors, and independent artists.
