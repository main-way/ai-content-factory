Title:Neural Networks as Linear Regression: An Introduction for Statisticians
View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:Neural networks are a commonly used prediction tool in computer science and statistics. However, the barrier to entry of this interesting field remains high, particularly for classical statisticians trained in a frequentist perspective. In this letter, we demystify neural networks by describing networks that approximate a linear regression and describe common customizations that provide a foundation for further study.
