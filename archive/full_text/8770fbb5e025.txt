Tom Griffiths, CEO of Gale Payments, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
Our market is the HSA and FSA payments space, working both online via e-commerce and in-store. Right now, there’s roughly $200B sitting in HSAs and FSAs and about $100B a year in spend. By 2035, there’ll be close to $1B in HSAs and FSAs and somewhere near $500M in annual spend. The No. 1 problem our company, Gale, solves today is that it’s very difficult for non-healthcare merchants to accept HSA and FSA payments. We make that easy to do compliantly so that these merchants can message their users with which products are eligible and accept that form of payment, essentially growing their sales.
