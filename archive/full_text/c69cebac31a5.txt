Kelz Bethel, Cofounder & CEO of Recess Labs, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
Recess focuses on frontline workers in this industry. There are over 214M frontline workers who face the risk of trauma exposure on the job. This includes doctors, nurses, teachers, police officers, and social workers. We are offering a B2B solution, so we are focusing on organizations in health, education, and public safety that hire these workers. There are about 2.2M organizations in health, education, and public safety that experience the problem that Recess is focusing on. From a market-size perspective, that gives Recess a TAM (total addressable market) of $79B. Our SAM (serviceable addressable market), however, focuses on frontline institutions that are already investing in mental health, training, and the implementation of trauma-informed care practices. More and more organizations are implementing trauma-informed training and workforce solutions, and this trend is growing at a rate of 17.5% per year. We are looking at organizations that have already implemented some of these training and awareness systems, and want a solution that provides frontline employees with an actionable, insightful tool so that they can reprocess some of the trauma outside of awareness. And that’s about $900M.
