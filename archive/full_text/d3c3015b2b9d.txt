After receiving some stellar critical reviews, including from CNET's Aaron Pruner, Project Hail Mary is rocketing to the MGM Plus streaming service.
The sci-fi movie stars Ryan Gosling as Ryland Grace, a high school teacher who makes an unexpected friend while alone on a spaceship on an outer-space mission to save humanity. The movie, based on Andy Weir's 2021 novel of the same name, premiered in theaters in March.
Project Hail Mary is directed by Phil Lord and Christopher Miller (The Lego Movie) and written by Drew Goddard (The Martian). The movie also stars Sandra Hüller (Anatomy of a Fall). If you want to stream the highly rated comedy on MGM Plus, here's what you need to know.
When to stream Project Hail Mary on MGM Plus
Project Hail Mary will launch on the MGM Plus streaming app on Thursday, June 18.
