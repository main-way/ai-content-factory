When to watch more Love Island USA season 8
A new episode of Love Island USA season 8 premieres on Peacock on Thursday, June 11, at 9 p.m. ET (6 p.m. PT).
In general, you can watch more Love Island USA programming every day except Wednesdays while the season is debuting. The show typically skips Wednesdays, and Saturdays are for Aftersun, which will kick off on Saturday, June 13.
Peacock's subscriptions include Premium, Premium Plus and Select. The $8-per-month Select tier provides seasons of NBC and Bravo titles like The Office and The Real Housewives, but it doesn't offer original Peacock shows like Love Island USA. You'll need Premium, which costs $11 per month, or Premium Plus, which runs $17 per month, to binge the popular summer reality show.
