Title:GC-LoRA: Gated Convolutional LoRA for Parameter-Efficient Acoustic Adaptation
View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:Transformer-based Speech Foundation Models excel in most Automatic Speech Recognition tasks but often suffer performance degradation when applied to domains with mismatched acoustic characteristics. While Parameter Efficient Fine-Tuning (PEFT) methods, such as Low-Rank Adaptation (LoRA), adjust global attention, they lack the local context modeling crucial for capturing domain-specific variations. We propose GC-LoRA, a novel adapter architecture that injects Conformer-style local convolutional processing into pretrained Transformer encoders. By integrating a lightweight adapter to encoder attention output projections, our method efficiently captures local acoustic dependencies without disrupting pretrained global representations. Experiments across diverse datasets (acoustically-degraded, bandlimited, dialectal, child) demonstrate the efficacy of our approach, achieving Word Error Rate (WER) reductions of up to 10.9% compared to baselines while adding minimal trainable parameters.
