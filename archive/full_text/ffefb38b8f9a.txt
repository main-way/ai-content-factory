Fabio Leto, CEO of SmartSEC, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
SmartSEC operates at the intersection of cybersecurity, IoT, digital security, and trust in digital infrastructures in general. Our addressable market is expanding across global markets, driven by digital transformation, connected devices, and regulatory compliance, which we estimate to be about $10B across Latin America and global emerging markets.
