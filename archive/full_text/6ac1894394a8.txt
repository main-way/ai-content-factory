Anchala Tomar, Founder and CEO of Multiply, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
Our market consists of content creators, and our industry is the creative economy. There are roughly 200M+ active digital creators worldwide, and, as per Goldman Sachs, it’s a $480B economy by 2027, with our estimated total addressable market at Euro 9.75B. We differentiate our company within the marketplace by multiplying the next income layer of the internet. While platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok help creators build an audience, very few earn through these platforms. So money is stuck in the top 1%, and that’s where we come into the picture. To trickle it down. We mostly compete with other platforms or tools that help creators make money. The way we do that is, rather than just depending on views or brand deals, or creators chasing virality or brand deals, we give them a new way to earn based on their engagement and how engaged their circle is. On the other side, brands are always looking for new customers. With our redemption infrastructure, we enable consumer brands to connect with verified creators through brand rewards. So as your circle engages more with your content, you start earning something we call time. As you reach a threshold, you can exchange that time for brand rewards. It’s a win-win because brands currently spend $700T on ads. Here, it’s more like performance marketing, where you pay us only when creators redeem your reward. So it’s a win-win for everyone.
