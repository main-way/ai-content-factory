Kevin Stein, CEO of Delos Insurance Solutions, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
Delos provides property insurance, home insurance, and commercial property insurance to folks who are in markets that are stressed because of catastrophes. Catastrophes, such as hurricanes and wildfires, have risen significantly in recent years. And Delos’ wildfire and hurricane modeling enables it to offer home and commercial insurance policies that are commensurate with the actual risk, as opposed to building in significant uncertainty due to the changing environment and the changing nature of these disasters. The market itself is $300B in premiums available to Delos, and we’re able to serve that population in a way that nobody else can because of our underlying modeling technology.
