Title:Improved Runtime Bound for the $(μ+ 1)$ EA on BinVal
View PDF HTML (experimental)Abstract:We study the $(\mu+1)$ EA on the Binary Value function BinVal. We show that it needs at most $O(\mu \log \mu \cdot n \log n)$ function evaluations to find the optimum when $\mu = o(n/\log n)$. This substantially improves upon the recent upper bound of $O(\mu^5 n \log(n/\mu^4))$ by Krejca, Neumann and Witt. Our results hold for several mutation operators including standard bit mutation. In particular, our bound implies that the $(\mu+1)$ EA is at most a factor $O(\log \mu \cdot \log n)$ slower on BinVal than on OneMax.
