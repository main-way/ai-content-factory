6 million+ page views per month from 1 million+ unique visitors
65% of the platform migrated from Pages Router to App Router in 7 days
Fern helps companies ship developer documentation and SDKs, running customer docs for Webflow, ElevenLabs, and others across custom domains from a single Next.js app on Vercel. Mid-migration from the Pages Router to the App Router, the team moved 65% of the platform in seven days instead of taking on a months-long engineering project. Today Fern deploys multiple times a day, with deploys down to five minutes and page load times cut by up to 80%.
