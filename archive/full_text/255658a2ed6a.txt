Aleksandr Abalakin, Chief Commercial Officer and Chief Marketing Officer at DefendSphere, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
We work in the European cybersecurity and JRC (the European Union’s Joint Research Centre, which maps cybersecurity standards) market, which is growing fast due to regulations like NIS2 (Network and Information Security 2, a directive harmonizing cybersecurity across the European Union) and DORA (the European Union Digital Operational Resilience Act). Today, the problem is quite simple. Large enterprise platforms are too heavy and too expensive for small and medium businesses. On the other hand, technical scanners don’t cover the legal side of compliance. DefendSphere sits in between. We combine legal requirements with real technical security checks. In reality, we mostly compete with manual consultants and with some automation tools that are not fully adapted to European regulations. Our difference is that we provide actual technical evidence of compliance through our own AI-driven scanning engine built specifically for the European market.
