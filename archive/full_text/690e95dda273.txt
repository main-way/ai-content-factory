Konrad Latowski, CEO of RIFFSEC, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
RIFFSEC operates in what we define as the cyber early warning platform market, built at the intersection of cyber threat intelligence and attack surface management. We started in anti-phishing and takedown services, expanded into CTI (cyber threat intelligence), and are now building toward a unified platform that helps organizations detect external signals early before they turn into operational, reputational, or regulatory crises. From a market perspective, we see this as a broad and growing space that includes CTI, external exposure monitoring, digital risk detection, and ASM (attack surface management). Rather than treating these as separate categories, we believe customers increasingly want one operational layer that connects threat visibility with real business context. Our focus is Europe first, with strong coverage of CEE and selected Middle Eastern markets, including Poland, the Czech Republic, Romania, Croatia, and Turkey.
