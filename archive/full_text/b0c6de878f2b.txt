Romain Brabant, CEO of Anxiety Checklist, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
We operate in the digital mental health market, specifically anxiety and panic management. Anxiety disorders affect hundreds of millions of people globally, some data says 300 million people, and the digital mental health market is already over $20 billion and growing fast. Our focus is on people who are anxious enough to seek help but not ready or able to start therapy because of the price. What makes us different is that we start within the moment we leave. When someone is anxious or panicking, we help them calm first, then understand what’s happening with the Anxiety University, and only after that, work on long-term change using simple CBT-style tools and education. CBT stands for Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. We most often compete with meditation apps like Calm or Headspace, and anxiety-specific apps like There. But unlike them, we combine immediate relief, education, and structured tools into one coherent experience designed specifically for anxiety.
