Armand Sanchez, Director of Product at NinjaTech AI, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
We operate in the autonomous AI workforce market, which is the convergence of AI agents, enterprise automation, and cloud computing that is fundamentally redefining how work is done. Our market isn’t just AI chatbots or copilots. We sit at the intersection of three massive overlapping spaces: AI agents and agentic AI platforms, enterprise business process automation, and cloud-based workforce augmentation. We think of it as the market for AI that doesn’t just advise, it actually executes.
