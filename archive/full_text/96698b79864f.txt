Zelda Darling, Editor in Chief of Nerdsleaze, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
We created Nerdsleaze because I wanted to focus on points that the algorithm wouldn’t snatch. Little did I know that with my first click to post, I would be a part of a market that’s worth over $150B. The internet publishing market includes known giants like The Verge, TechCrunch, and Polygon, along with the bright minds of Substack. However, I continued to focus on the outliers and curated a space that serves competent adults globally. They live in the real world, and they have real lives to manage.
