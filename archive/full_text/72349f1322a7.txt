Sid Garimella, CEO of Rulebricks, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
Our TAM is about $300-500M. This is going roughly off of what some of the bigger rule and decision engines in the space are seeing, plus where we see the market being able to grow when we make rule engines and decision engines more accessible to a larger audience. The No. 1 problem we solve for our customers is the ability to take deeply embedded decisions that are technically only editable by engineering resources and to surface them within spreadsheet user interfaces that allow domain owners, process owners, domain experts, and even clients to edit the logic that pertains to an automated decision.
