Jeremy Friese, Founder and CEO of Humata Health, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
Here at Humata Health, we’re a one-trick pony. All we think about all day, every day is the problem of prior authorization. It’s a problem that plagues, most importantly, patients. But it’s a problem, as we like to say, on both sides of the fax machine, with both providers and payers. And so here at Humata, we are uniquely situated to solve this problem for all players in the ecosystem — for providers, payers, pharma, and also, consequently, for patients. The total addressable market for prior authorization is north of $30B. This includes the current level of prior authorization across commercial and Medicare Advantage books of business, with likely expansion into Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) as things progress.
