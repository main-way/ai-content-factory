Alex Peleg, CEO and Co-Founder to Cyn.AI, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market and where does your company fit into that space?
From where I’m sitting as a co-founder, we don’t just see ourselves as another security vendor. We are part of a shift toward synthetic intelligence in the enterprise. This is how we define our specific landscapes and our wedge within them. Our TAM (total addressable market) is in the broader cybersecurity market. The market is around $300B+ globally, but our specific TAM is at the intersection of AI-driven security operations, the SecOps part, and exposure management. We estimate the serviceable market for the autonomous security agent to be roughly $15B to $20B, growing at 25% yearly. Our focus is on the automation gap; the $1T spent on detection tools that still require human labor to fix the problem is where we are putting our weight.
