Nicholas Singh, Founder & CEO of MindFit, tells CB Insights how they view the market, customer needs, and their company.
How do you define your market, and where does your company fit into that space?
Estimated total addressable market — the way that I think about it is, you look at how many US adult workforce members are seeking preventative mental health and mental fitness solutions. According to the American Psychological Association’s annual survey called Stress in America, about two-thirds of the US adult workforce are in this bucket called silent sufferers.
